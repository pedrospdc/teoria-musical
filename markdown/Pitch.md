##  Section 1.1 Pitch

Pitch has to do with notes. On the piano there are 88 notes. As you move to the right on a piano, the notes sound higher. Notes sound lower as you move to the left. Try it on a piano with a smaller range at [the following website](http://www.musictheory.net/piano).

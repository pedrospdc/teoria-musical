##  Section 29.3 Voice Leading the Neapolitan Chord

There are two rules for voice leading [the \\(\left.\text{N}^{6}\right.\\) chord](NeapolitanIntroduction.html "Section 20.1: The Neapolitan Chord") in four parts: 

  1. Double the bass (the third) 

  2. Resolve ♭\\(\hat{2} \\) _down_ to the nearest note in the next chord.

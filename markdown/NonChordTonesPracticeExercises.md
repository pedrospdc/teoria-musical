##  Exercises 10.12 Practice Exercises

### 1.

Fill in the blanks in the following table. 

_Non-Chord Tone Type_ | _Approached by_ | _Left by_  
---|---|---  
| same tone | step up  
|  | leap in opposite direction  
| step | step in same direction  
| leap |   
  
Answer.

_Non-Chord Tone Type_ | _Approached by_ | _Left by_  
---|---|---  
_RETARDATION_ | same tone | step up  
_ESCAPE TONE_ | _STEP_ | leap in opposite direction  
_PASSING TONE_ | step | step in same direction  
_APPOGGIATURA_ | leap | _STEP_

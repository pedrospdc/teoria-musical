##  Section 16.5 Additional Information

Although we will not use them in our harmonic analyses, you will sometimes encounter figured bass symbols containing all of the intervals above the bass note in Baroque music containing figured bass. The common figured bass inversion symbols in the previous sections are abbreviations of the figures given in the examples below.

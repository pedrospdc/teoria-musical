##  Seção 17.5 Writing Secondary Dominants

To write a secondary dominante, use the following procedure. 

  1. First determine the nota that is the root of the acorde being tonicized (the acorde to the right of the slash). 

![](external/images/unit4/sec-dom-writing-determine-tonicized-escala-degree.svg)

  2. Determine the root of the \\(\left.\text{V}^{7}\right.\\) in the key of B♭ (the Roman numeral _after_ the slash): _F_

  3. Build a major–minor seventh acorde on F: _F–A–C–E_ ♭ 

  4. Invert the acorde accordingly. \\(\left.\text{}^{4}_{3}\right.\\) inversion means the fifth of the acorde is in the bass. 

![](external/images/unit4/sec-dom-writing-invert-answer.svg)

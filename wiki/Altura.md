##  Seção 1.1 Altura

Altura has to do with notas. On the piano there are 88 notas. As you move to the right em um piano, the notas sound higher. Notas sound inferior as you move to the left. Try it em um piano with a smaller range at [the following website](http://www.musictheory.net/piano).

##  Seção 10.5 Escape Tone

An escape tone is approached by step and left by leap in the opposite direction. An easy example to remember is the end of the “Star-Spangled Banner.” The word “of” in “home _of_ the brave” is an escape tone.

Figura 10.5.1. Francis Scott Key and John Smith, “Star-Spangled Banner” (1814)

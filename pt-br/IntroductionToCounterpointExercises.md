##  Exercises 30.9 Exercícios Práticos

Species Contraponto: Day One 

### 

First and Second Species Exercises 

#### 1.

For each exercise below, write first species (nota-against-nota) contraponto. Write the intervallic distância from the cantus firmus (“ _c.f._ ”) to the contraponto in the blanks above the pauta. Remember to begin and end with an oitava or unison, to proceed to the last unison by step in both voices, and use only consonances (1, 3, 5, 6, 8, 10).

##  Seção 26.12 Summary of Doubling Rules for Tríades

To generalize, here are the doubling rules for condução de vozes tríades in root position, first inversion, and second inversion: 

  * Root Position Acordes: DOUBLE THE BASS 

  * First-Inversion Acordes: DO NOT DOUBLE THE BASS

    * Exception: \\(\left.\text{vii}^{\circ}{}^{6}\right.\\)and \\(\left.\text{ii}^{\circ}{}^{6}\right.\\): DOUBLE THE BASS 

    * Exception: Consecutive first inversion acordes—alternate between DOUBLE THE BASS and DO NOT DOUBLE THE BASS 

  * Second-Inversion Acordes: DOUBLE THE BASS

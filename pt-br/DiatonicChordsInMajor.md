##  Seção 7.2 Diatonic Acordes in Major

Observe the padrão of diatonic acordes in major represented by Roman numerals. (Diatonic means notas _within_ a armadura de clave and can be contrasted with the term “chromatic.”) One sees the padrão M-m-m-M-M-m-\\(\left.\text{}^{\circ}{}\right.\\) in tríade quality.

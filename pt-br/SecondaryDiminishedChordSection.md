##  Seção 18.1 Secondary Diminished Acordes

The \\(\left.\text{vii}^{\circ}{}\right.\\) acorde has dominante function (see the [Harmonic Function Flowchart](HarmonicFunction.html#HarmonicFlowchartInMajor "Figura 9.4.2")). Since acordes of dominante function typically resolve to acordes of tônica function, diminished tríades as well as half–diminished and fully–diminished seventh acordes occur as secondary diminished acordes.
